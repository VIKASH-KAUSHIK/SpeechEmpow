About Us

Learn more about our mission and commitment to speech therapy excellence

Our Story

SpeechEmpow was founded in 2020 by a team of certified speech-language pathologists who recognized the need for more accessible speech therapy services. Our founders experienced firsthand the challenges many individuals face when seeking quality speech therapy - from long wait times to geographical limitations and high costs.

With a commitment to breaking down these barriers, we created an innovative platform that connects clients with qualified speech therapists through secure online sessions. Our goal is to make speech therapy accessible to everyone, regardless of location or circumstances.

Since our launch, we've helped thousands of individuals improve their communication skills and gain confidence in their speech. We continue to expand our services and refine our approach based on client feedback and the latest research in speech therapy.

Our Mission

At SpeechEmpow, our mission is to transform lives through accessible, high-quality speech therapy. We strive to empower individuals of all ages to overcome speech and communication challenges by providing personalized therapy solutions, innovative tools, and unwavering support.

We believe that everyone deserves the opportunity to express themselves clearly and confidently. By removing barriers to speech therapy services, we aim to help our clients achieve their communication goals and enhance their quality of life.

Our Vision

We envision a world where speech therapy is readily available to anyone who needs it, regardless of geographical location or financial constraints. Our vision is to be the leading online platform for speech therapy services, known for excellence, innovation, and client-centered care.

We aspire to continuously expand our reach, develop cutting-edge tools, and foster a community where individuals can support each other on their speech improvement journeys.
