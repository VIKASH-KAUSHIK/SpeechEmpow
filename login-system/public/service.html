How We Deliver Therapy

1 Live Video Sessions

Connect with your therapist face-to-face through our secure, HIPAA-compliant video platform. Perfect for interactive therapy where visual cues and feedback are important.

  • HD video and clear audio
  • Screen sharing for therapy materials
  • Session recording (with permission)

2 Live Chat Support

Get instant text-based support from your therapist for quick questions, follow-ups, and practice reminders. Ideal for on-the-go communication between sessions.

  • Secure messaging
  • File and audio sharing
  • Response notifications

3 Speech Analytics

Utilize our AI-powered speech analysis tools to track progress, identify patterns, and receive automated feedback on practice exercises between sessions.

  • Pronunciation scoring
  • Progress tracking
  • Customized practice recommendations
